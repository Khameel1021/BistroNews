Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cookingtechnique called sous vide. In sous videcooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into awater bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, JeffPotter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.
